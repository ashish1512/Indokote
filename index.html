Welcome to Indokote


High Protective Coatings

Indokote's protective coatings cover a wide range of applications in the industrial world, from Oil Refinaries and Power Generation to Infrastructure and light industry.

We've got what you need!

Indokote initially manufactures industrial paints to local Agriculture Implements, Transformer and Pre Engineering Building Industries. Industrial paints based on Alkyds, Acrylic, Epoxies and Polyurethanes are well known by the company, but it was needed to research exhaustively to meet diverse demands on anticorrosive and maintenance coatings from Petrochemical, Transformers, Heavy Infrastructure and Power Generation complexes.

The company started with an extensive research on paint systems based on epoxies, polyurethanes, vinyl, polyaspartic, and silicate series. Now the company research and development unit is equipped with advanced devices and continuously research to improve all industrial and anticorrosive coating systems. With a wide product range and efficient R&D team, we work with you to tailor each solution to meet the specific requirements of the environment and industry you operate in.
